WELCOME TO PAGBILAO

BAYAN NG MANANALO

The town of Pagbilao was founded in the early 17th century by a couple named Pablo and Rita. They were pioneers who ventured into the wilderness and settled in the area. The name Pagbilao comes from a misunderstanding between the original settlers and Spanish missionaries. The settlers' words for "bamboo bed" (papag) and "winnowing basket" (bilao) were combined by the Spanish to create the town's name (PAGBILAO).
